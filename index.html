What is MoraXtreme 7.0?

Moraxtreme 7.0 is the sequel of the annual hackathon series organized by the IEEE computer society University of Moratuwa. This is solely conducted for Inter-University coders to show their expertise in algorithmic and mathematical problem solving and with the intention of enhancing the number of the top ranks of Sri Lanka globally in IEEEXtreme, the most exciting international coding competition. A 12-hour online battle among teams to acknowledge the conundrum and crack the code to flaunt in the competition. All the challenges are developed by our highly skilled team of top-ranked code experts in the coding history of IEEEXtreme and hosted in the HackerRank platform. Grab this precious opportunity to showcase your problem-solving skills. Let the best team acquire the victory.
